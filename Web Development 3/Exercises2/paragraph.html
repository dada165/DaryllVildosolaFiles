In the beginning of my journey to become a college student, I thought that being in College is easy like in Highscool.

Starting at college without all of my friends was something that was worrying me. But it was finally here, today was the day that I started to study at University Of San Carlos Talamban Campus, a known school in Cebu. My emotions were all over the place. I did not know whether to laugh or cry. I felt scared, excited, worried and nervous, all at the same time. My stomach was turning at the thought of having to go to a new place, where everything was unfamiliar to me, and also without my parents beside me. I felt like I needed their presence, but it was down to me to find out how to manage my life away from them.
